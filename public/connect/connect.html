Supplies Needed

Your generous donations help us continue to care for our furry friends. Here’s a list of supplies we currently need:

  • Cat Food (Canned and Dry)
  • Litter and Litter Boxes
  • Toys and Scratch Posts
  • Beds and Blankets
  • Cleaning Supplies (Disinfectants, Paper Towels, etc.)
  • Pet Carriers

If you’d like to donate any of these items, feel free to drop them off at our center during business hours.
