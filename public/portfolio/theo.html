Meet Theo

This is Theo, a sweet 10-year-old tabby cat who is looking for a calm and loving home. Theo has a gentle nature and loves curling up in cozy spots for long naps. He may be a little slower than some of the younger cats, but he has a heart full of love to give. Theo is independent but enjoys gentle affection from his people. He’s a perfect companion for someone looking for a low-energy, easy-going cat to share their life with. Despite his age, Theo is in great health and is ready to spend his golden years with a family who will appreciate his quiet charm.
