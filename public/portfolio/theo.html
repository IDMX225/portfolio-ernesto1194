Meet Theo

Leo is a 3-year-old tabby with a beautiful coat and golden eyes that will steal your heart. He’s full of energy and loves to play—whether it’s chasing toys or exploring every corner of the house. But after a good play session, Leo loves nothing more than curling up beside you for some quiet cuddle time, purring contentedly as he relaxes.

Social and affectionate, Leo enjoys being around his humans and will happily sit by your side or hop on your lap for attention. He’s a bit chatty, especially when it’s time for treats, and will make sure to keep you entertained with his sweet meows.

Leo would thrive in an active home where he can get plenty of playtime and affection. If you're looking for a fun, loving cat to be your companion, Leo is ready to meet you!
