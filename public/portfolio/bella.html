Meet Bella

Bella is a 2-year-old Domestic Shorthair with stunning brown and black tabby stripes. She’s a curious and playful cat who loves to explore, chase toys, and keep herself entertained. Once her adventures are over, she’s quick to curl up next to you for some quiet cuddle time.

Bella gets along well with other cats and would thrive in a home where she can receive plenty of attention and playtime. If you're looking for a fun, affectionate companion who will bring joy and love to your home, Bella is ready to meet you!
