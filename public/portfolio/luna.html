Meet Luna

Luna is a 3-year-old Siamese with creamy fur and captivating blue eyes. She's social, playful, and loves to chat, especially when she’s seeking attention. Luna thrives in a home where she’s the center of attention and enjoys interacting with her people.

Luna is adaptable and can easily fit into a household with other pets. If you’re looking for a talkative, affectionate cat with plenty of personality, Luna could be your perfect match!
