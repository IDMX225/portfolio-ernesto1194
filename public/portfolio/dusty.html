Meet Dusty

Dusty is a stunning 5-year-old Russian Blue with a soft, silvery-blue coat. Known for his quiet and intelligent nature, Dusty can be shy at first but quickly warms up to those who earn his trust. Once he’s comfortable, he’ll shower you with affection, purring contently as he snuggles beside you.

Dusty thrives in a peaceful, loving home where he can relax and enjoy time with his people. If you're looking for a beautiful, loyal companion who loves cuddles but also enjoys his personal space, Dusty is the perfect match.
