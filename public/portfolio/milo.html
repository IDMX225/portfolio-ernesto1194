Meet Milo

Milo is a 4-year-old Maine Coon with a thick, luxurious coat and tufted ears that give him a regal appearance. Despite his majestic looks, Milo is incredibly gentle and loving, with a "dog-like" personality. He enjoys being the center of attention and will happily follow you around, always seeking affection and companionship.

Milo loves being brushed and thrives in a home where he can receive plenty of love and care. If you’re looking for a big-hearted, affectionate cat who will be your loyal companion, Milo is the perfect match!
