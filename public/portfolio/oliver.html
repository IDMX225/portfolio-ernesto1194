Meet Oliver

This is Oliver, a 3-year-old playful tabby cat with a striking pattern of orange and brown stripes. Known for his outgoing, friendly nature, Oliver loves to engage with his people and is always the first to greet you with a purr and a playful bounce. He enjoys long play sessions with his favorite toys, but when he's not playing, he loves curling up in a sunny spot for a nap. Oliver is looking for an active home where he can get plenty of attention and playtime. If you're looking for a fun-loving, affectionate cat who’s sure to bring joy to your life, Oliver might be the perfect match.
